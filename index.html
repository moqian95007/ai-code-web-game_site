About MonsterSurvivors

Game Overview

MonsterSurvivors is an engaging survival game that puts your strategic thinking and reflexes to the test. As a lone survivor, you must fend off waves of increasingly challenging monsters while upgrading your abilities and equipment.

Key Features

  • Dynamic wave-based gameplay
  • Character progression system
  • Diverse monster types
  • Strategic combat mechanics
  • Regular updates and new content

How to Play

In MonsterSurvivors, your goal is to survive as long as possible against waves of monsters. Here's what you need to know:

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Click or press space to attack
  • Collect experience points to level up
  • Choose upgrades between waves to strengthen your character
  • Stay alive as long as possible to achieve high scores
